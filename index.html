About

An eye for what others walk past.

I'm Raquel — a vintage collector based in Austin, TX with an eye for finding exceptional pieces that most people walk right past. I specialize in sourcing high-quality vintage furniture and home goods: the kind that retail for thousands but deserve to be found, loved, and lived with.

Whether you're an interior designer building a client's space or simply looking for something one-of-a-kind, I find it for you.
